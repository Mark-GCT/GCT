ABOUT GATEWAY CORNERSTONE

VETERAN-OWNED FAITH DRIVEN WEB DESIGN IN ST. LOUIS & NATIONWIDE

Hi, I'm Mark Elmore — founder of Gateway Cornerstone Web Design and a web developer with over 15 years in IT.

In that time, I've done it all: desktop support, large-scale infrastructure projects, and full website builds. But it was development that stuck. It gave me the flexibility to show up fully for what matters most to me — my family, my community, and my faith. Those three things don't just inform my personal life; they shape every professional decision I make. Faith, integrity, and excellence aren't a tagline here. They're the foundation.

So why did I launch Gateway Cornerstone? Because I kept seeing the same problem everywhere I looked.

Businesses and organizations would pour time into a DIY website builder, or hand their brand over to a generic WordPress theme — and end up with something slow, forgettable, and vulnerable to security threats. They weren't getting a competitive tool. They were getting a digital placeholder.

You deserve better than that.

At Gateway Cornerstone, I work with a trusted network of independent specialists — designers, strategists, and technical experts — brought in to match the specific needs of your project. This means you get focused, senior-level talent without the overhead of a large agency, and you always have a direct line to me.

I'm especially passionate about partnering with small businesses and purpose-driven organizations — including faith-based nonprofits, ministries, and churches. I understand your goals aren't always purely commercial, and your budget rarely is either. You need a website that works as hard as your mission does, and I know how to build that.

A great website isn't just an online presence. It's a handshake, a first impression, and your hardest-working team member — available 24/7.

Are you ready to build something that actually works for you? Let's talk.
