ABOUT GATEWAY CORNERSTONE

ENTERPRISE IT. SMALL BUSINESS BUILT. VETERAN OWNED.

Hi, I'm Mark Elmore — founder of Gateway Cornerstone Technologies and an IT professional with over 15 years of enterprise IT experience. I spent years inside large-scale IT operations and saw firsthand what small businesses were missing. The same systems protecting Fortune 500 companies, running their infrastructure, and securing their data were completely out of reach for small and mid-size organizations — priced out, over-engineered, or buried in jargon they shouldn't have to navigate alone. That gap is exactly why GCT exists.


WHO WE ARE

YOUR IT PARTNER. NOT JUST A VENDOR.


Gateway Cornerstone Technologies is a veteran-owned managed service provider (MSP) based in the STL Metro East region, serving small and mid-size businesses across Illinois and the greater St. Louis area. We deliver enterprise-grade IT infrastructure, cybersecurity, and emerging AI services — fully tailored to organizations that don't have the budget or bandwidth for an in-house IT department. We don't do break/fix. We don't show up after something goes wrong and hand you an invoice. We operate as a true extension of your team — proactive, invested, and accountable — so that IT becomes a growth driver instead of a recurring headache.

GCT helps small businesses identify, implement, and operationalize AI tools — from staff training and process automation to fully deployed AI agents that work around the clock. If your competitors are exploring AI, now is the time to move.
