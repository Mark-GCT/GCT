AI tools are everywhere, but most small business owners don't have the time to test a dozen platforms, figure out which one fits their workflow, or troubleshoot integrations when something breaks. Getting AI to actually work inside a real business, with real data and real staff, takes planning, setup, and testing that most owners don't have bandwidth for.
